Visit Vandalur

"Welcome to Vandalur, a serene and rapidly growing suburb of Chennai, known for its tranquil surroundings and connectivity to both city life and nature."

Top Three Activities to do in Vandalur

Nandhivaram Lake

A peaceful spot for nature enthusiasts and bird watchers, offering scenic views and a relaxing atmosphere.

Arignar Anna Zoological Park (Vandalur Zoo)

Located nearby, it’s one of India’s largest zoos, housing a diverse range of wildlife and perfect for a family outing.

Vandalur Hill Temple

A serene and less-crowded temple, providing a panoramic view of the surrounding areas and a quiet spot for spiritual seekers.
